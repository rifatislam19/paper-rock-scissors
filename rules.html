Rules

The game of P, R, S is based on trust, skill and determination. The rules are:
    Each player must make a fist with one hand and hold the other open, palm upward.
    Each player must tap their fists in their open palms once, twice, and on the third time form either paper, rock, or scissors.
    A rock is formed by keeping the hand in a fist.
    A sheet of paper is formed by holding the hand flat, palm down.
    A pair of scissors is formed by extending the first two fingers and holding them apart.
    If the same item is thrown, it's a tie. Otherwise, rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock.
For a more detailed list of rules, go to the World RPS Society website.
